What is an archive?

Archives are the documentary by product of human activity retained for their long term value. Usually archive is keeping those record for evidence.

They are contemporary records that created by individuals and organization and it will be keep in archive. It is because archive will manage them according to the law

The records can come from many type of forms such as written,digital and so on. It based on how the organization or indovidual want the records in which forms.

There are four characteristics of archive resource consist of authenticity,reliability,integrity,usability.
